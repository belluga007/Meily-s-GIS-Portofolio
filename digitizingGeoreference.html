Georeference and Digitize Old Map 1945
Short Description
Tools : Digitation, ArcMap
Georeferencing and digitization are two key processes for converting paper maps or static images into digital data usable in geographic information systems (GIS). I came up with the idea of ​using an old map from 1945, dating back to the colonial era, to compare it to the current situation. I discovered many interesting things about the many changes that have occurred over the past eight decades. The most significant change is land use, indicating that Jakarta has experienced massive growth.
Data
  1. Indonesia Topographic Map, Bantul Regency, Yogyakarta Special Region, 2019, Indonesia Geospatial
  2. Road Shapefile, 2019
  3. Tsunami Evacuation Plan Map For Bantul Regency, Yogyakarta, 2010
Deficiencies and possible improvements
  1. Unkown or old projection system becomes a very critical issue in georeferencing and digitizing old maps
  2. Lack of understanding about how to use old projection or different projection or transformation systems that is not familiar
  3. This process is a basic process in GIS capabilities but requires high patience and precision.
