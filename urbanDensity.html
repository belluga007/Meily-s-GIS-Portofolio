Urban Density Analysis
Short Description
Tools : Google Earth Engine
From Landsat 9 surface reflectance to generate NDBI (Normalized Difference Built-up Index) and NDBR2 (Normalized Burn Ratio 2) to mask the built-up area. The built-up area is generated using a relational operation threshold from NBDI and NBR2. Finally, to generate urban density by using the focal or convolution operation for 3 neighborhood pixels to calculate the mean value. The result is the urban density index.
Tools and Data
Tools : Google Earth Engine, ArcMap, Excel Method : NDBI and NDBR2
